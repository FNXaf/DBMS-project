Our Story

Nestled in the quiet outskirts of the city, Meowtopia began as a humble dream in 2018 — a small cottage with three rescued kittens and one determined founder who believed every cat deserves a second chance at love.

What started as weekend fostering quickly blossomed into a full sanctuary. Today, Meowtopia is home to over a hundred cats of every breed, colour, and personality — each one waiting for the right human to walk through our doors.

We don't just rehome cats. We match souls. Our adoption counsellors spend time getting to know both you and our residents, ensuring every pairing is as natural as a cat choosing the warmest spot on the sofa.

Our on-site veterinary clinic provides full health checks, vaccinations, and spay/neuter procedures before any adoption — because a healthy cat is a happy cat. Every feline that leaves Meowtopia is microchipped, dewormed, and loved.

We believe in transparency and trust. Every cat's profile on our platform shows their breed, age, vaccination status, and personality — so you know exactly who you're welcoming into your family before you even visit.

Whether you're here to adopt your first companion or simply lose yourself in the gentle purring of a hundred happy cats — welcome to Meowtopia. We've been expecting you.
